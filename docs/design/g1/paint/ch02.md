## §3 · Bewitchment concept

OSWIN shuffled the zoo: every animal is in the WRONG place and the signs lie („There
is a lion in the monkey house" — the corpus's own wrong-line is the world's state).
The bewitched TREES (Koki's cast) grew through the paths and carry the wrong signs.
`goalDe`: „Bring die Tiere an ihre richtigen Plätze — sag, WO sie sind, dann finden
sie heim." `whyDe`: „Jedes Tier am richtigen Platz macht den Zoo wieder froh."
Restoration: corrected enclosures light up; their animal does a happy loop.
