## §3 · Bewitchment concept

OSWIN stole the beat: the band's song fell apart into stray notes, and the
instruments — huge and alive in the painted hills — argue over WHOSE music it is
(„It's MY guitar!" — the corpus's wrong claim is the world's quarrel). Nobody can
play; everybody CAN'T. `goalDe`: „Sag, wer was spielen kann, und wem was gehört —
dann findet die Band ihren Song wieder." `whyDe`: „Jede richtige Zeile bringt einen
Takt der Musik zurück." Restoration: the world regains its soundtrack layer by
layer — each restored instrument ADDS ITS TRACK to the chapter's music (audible
restoration; the anti-stale law applied to sound).

## §6 · Guardian arena — das wilde Schlagzeug + FAVOR POWER I

The drum kit plays ITSELF — furious, arrhythmic, alone at the back of the stage.
- Pattern: it hurls cymbal discs (shimmer telegraph) and stomps beat-shockwaves
  (jump windows ON the beat — the arena teaches rhythm literally); every 2nd knot a
  sour-note swarm pulse.
- **Deflect:** fist a cymbal back into the kit — the crash knocks it off-beat;
  stagger.
- **Counter-window tasks:** can/can't rounds („It can play loud. It can't stop!") ·
  possessive fixes („It's Anna's drum!" per corpus) · pronoun swaps · melody-order
  quickfires (arrange a bar).
- **Cry + console:** the kit collapses mid-crash — it can KEEP a beat but it can't
  play a SONG alone; it was drumming to call the band back. The hero sits and claps
  the missing off-beat with it (choice-less warm beat). Ally: the camp gains its
  drummer — the camp scene gets a live beat from here on.
- **★ FAVOR POWER I — „der Taktsprung" (the beat-jump):** the consoled kit gifts a
  pocket drum: ON the beat, a jump from any drum/bounce surface launches HIGHER
  (a rhythm-timed super-bounce; deliberately vertical — it unlocks ch06's tall play
  and retro-opens tall secrets = backtrack beat 2, doc 31 §4). Fictionally earned,
  never granted (the favor economy law).
- Victory: the full band setup fills in, instrument by instrument; the chapter's
  soundtrack completes (the §3 restoration pays off at maximum here).
