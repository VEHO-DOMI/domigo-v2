## 4 · Level design (v3 layout; engine set-pieces land at assembly)
- Flow: schoolyard (slopes, alphabet letters strewn as pickups, object-battle encounters
  roaming) → flagpole/pencil-pole climb → porch door `1` → **Klassenzimmer wing = the
  RESTORATION ROOM** (drained; the midnight-classroom set-piece) → facade terraces → attic
  stairs blocked by the **NUMBER SWARM** → **Dachboden wing = the COMMAND-DUEL arena** (the
  ghost-student) → gable → the Schlinger's door (composition boss) → dialogue finale.
- The two former "Siegel" become the two verknotete Stundenseiten: one released by completing
  the restoration room, one by the command duel (no abstract posts).
- Collectible: `letters` = the alphabet (existing engine letters, re-fictioned; HUD counts
  „Buchstaben"). Hint object: GLÜHBUCHSTABEN (re-skin of the Glühwörter economy, doc 30 §5).
- Decor law (doc 30 §1.5): schoolyard fences, satchels, timetables, chalk scribbles — all
  Unit-1 objects; interior decor = SB p.14 classroom (board, projector, desks, sound system).
