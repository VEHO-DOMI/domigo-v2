## 4 · Level design (cookbook §7: multi-floor arena approach, ~45×40)
- **The hush**: sparse, vertical, NO letters, NO creatures after the first floor (the
  Well-of-Wishes doctrine) — the quietest level in the game. Climb the spine-bridge floors
  (poles + one slow mover, generous platforms; slopes as gentle page-curls), past unlit
  alcoves holding one Jona note per act recalled („Niemand liest mit mir." → „Pixel hat
  neben mir geschlafen." → „Ihr habt meine Seite schön gemacht.") — his arc in three papers.
- 2 seals only, UNGUARDED (free walk-up pickups — the fight is over; the book itself opens
  the door). 1 Glühwort, given by Pixel (she walks the last floor with you).
- Population: 2 walkers on floor one (E) that do not pursue; nothing else. No spikes.
- Secret: none. The level withholds nothing — that is its statement.

## 5 · THE RESCUE DUEL (inversion mode — bible §2b settled law)
- Script name: **Jona**. Intro: „Er steht an der Tür und hält sie zu. ‚Ich weiß nicht, was
  ich draußen machen soll.'" Outro: „Jona nimmt deine Hand. Die Tür geht auf. Die Seite
  atmet auf — und das ganze Buch mit ihr."
- NOT taunts — **doubts**, answered by play: „Draußen wartet niemand." · „Ich habe es
  vergessen." · „Was, wenn ich es wieder nicht kann?" Finn's helper line between knots:
  „Sag es ihm noch einmal. Er hört zu."
- Inversion rules (engine as built): hearts cannot be lost · Jona takes no damage number ·
  standing in the lane never hits · wrong-in-window only closes the window and the pattern
  repeats — the duel cannot be failed, only prolonged.
- 5 knots · pattern [1,1,0,2,1,0] (he mostly stays center — he is not attacking, he is
  holding on) · telegraph E1100/M900/S700 (slower than every guardian — grief is readable) ·
  windows E30/M25/S20 (the longest windows in the game).
- Task mix: going-to production ONLY, scaffolding down on wrong (typed → chips), each window
  a first-person future sentence („I am going to…") — the player literally speaks Jona's
  future until s005's question has its answer.
