## 4 · Level design (build spec — cookbook §7 archetype "showcase traverse")
- Size ~90×26 (6×2.4 screens), horizontal traverse with **color-return set-pieces mid-level**:
  the level starts grey-washed; at three trigger lines a colorwave sweeps a zone back to full
  EGA (the `ch14_colorwave` strip) — the map's arc, played inside one level. Entry sentence:
  "We dance into the Colour Studio."
- Flow: splash-field approach (slopes) → **colorwave 1** → **2 seal wings via door pairs**
  (record room: turntable mover as rotating ride, seal 1 · gallery shaft: shelf climb +
  poles, seal 2) → **colorwave 2** → the motif parade → **colorwave 3** on the final ramp →
  the guardian's door one beat past.
- **The motif parade (cookbook §7: every earlier motif cameos once, ~one screen each):**
  enclosure point-room (ch02) · rigging pole (ch03) · one mood-switch platform (ch04) ·
  Sprungkissen bounce-line (ch05) · dim clue pocket (ch06) · short conveyor (ch07) ·
  washing-line one-way (ch08) · soft-drop shaft (ch09) · shelf-tower bite (ch10) · one
  fading Miragia platform (ch11) · letter-banquet pocket (ch12) · a ten-tile low/high split
  (ch13). Each cameo re-colors as the player clears it — the traverse IS the year.
- Verbs: slopes · poles (gallery shaft) · door pairs ×2 · 1 mover with a job (the turntable
  ferry) · drop-throughs · the ch11-cameo fade platform.
- Population (E, ~1/screen): walker ×2 · hopper ×1 · cushion ×1 · signature sending
  **Farb-Flatterklecks** ×3 (trails a colour ribbon; stunned, it briefly re-colors the tiles
  under its arc — the only creature that HELPS the colorwaves; three-beat grammar kept) ·
  guards: walker (record room), hopper (gallery). M adds flyer + walker; S adds parade thief.
- Hazard family: grey-wash pools (ink-pool grammar, re-skinned) — standing in one greys the
  hero for 2s (visual + a slow-shove; gentle, register-safe).
- Collectibles: letters along the parade (one cluster per cameo) · 3 Glühwörter (turntable
  center — mover-anchor line; gallery-top pocket — pogo-height hint; parade wall gap —
  shimmer hint) · helpers ×2 E.
- Secret: just past where the camera seems to stop, behind the ch12-cameo pocket → the little
  gallery — all thirteen earlier grounds in miniature, already warm (letters + 1 Glühwort;
  the reward is the view).

## 5 · Boss duel (author `ch14.boss.json` to this)
- Name: **Der Graumacher**. Intro: „Der Graumacher hält alle Lieblingssachen fest. Grau ist
  sicher, sagt er — was niemand liebt, kann niemand verlieren." Outro: „Ein wahres Wort nach
  dem anderen kommt die Farbe zurück — zuerst Grün. Der Graumacher blinzelt im Licht, und
  fast lächelt er. Die Seite atmet auf." Taunts: „Grau genügt!" · „Lieblingssachen? Nur
  geliehen!" · „Ohne Farben ist es schön still."
- 5 knots · pattern [1,2,0,1,0,2] · telegraph E950/M780/S620 (tell = a greyscale wave rolls
  across the arena floor toward the attack lane) · windows E25/M20/S15.
- Task mix: past-simple-irregular + past-simple-negative, alternated with favourites-vocab
  production. CLT: every knot = one grey favourite — say truly what was („I saw it, I had
  it") and what wasn't („he didn't stop liking it"), and its colour walks home; the LAST
  knot returns green — the duel ends on Jona's colour.
