## 4 · Level design (build spec — cookbook §7 archetype "the gauntlet")
- Size ~100×22 (6.7×2 screens) — Isle-of-Fire grammar: **safe slow LOW path** (ground route)
  vs **risky fast HIGH path** (rooftop/hose-lines through the hazards); both converge at each
  wing. Entry sentence: "We race into the Rescue Station!"
- Flow: station yard (slopes) → the split → **3 seal wings via door pairs** (training tower:
  pole ladders, seal 1 · garage: engine bay, seal 2 · drill yard: mover crossing, seal 3) →
  convergence ramp along the rift's hushed, palest edge → the guardian's door one beat past.
- Verbs: poles as station ladders (Sam's climb, playable) · door pairs ×3 · 2 movers with
  jobs (ladder-lift over the tower shaft; stretcher-ferry to the drill-yard seal) ·
  drop-through hose-lines · slopes.
- Population (E, **densest in the campaign: 1.25/screen**): walker ×2 · hopper ×1 · cloud ×1
  · signature sending **Sirenen-Flatterklecks** ×3 (swoops ON the siren beat: rising-pitch
  tell → swoop → glide-safe — the pitch rehearses the duel's telegraph) · guards: walker
  (tower), hopper (garage), flyer (drill yard). M adds walker + cloud; S adds high-path thief.
- Hazard family: red-mark fire flickers (fire≈red-marks grammar) — ONLY on the high path.
  The rift itself is never a hazard (absence, not a trap).
- Collectibles: letters breadcrumb the LOW path (the calm route is the learning route) ·
  3 Glühwörter (tower-top pocket — pogo-height hint; garage ceiling pocket — the Sirenen-
  Flatterklecks' swoop arc points in; convergence wall gap — shimmer hint) · helpers ×2 E.
- Secret: a just-past-where-the-camera-seems-to-stop passage behind the garage → the quiet
  room (letters + 1 Glühwort, the station's rest corner — calm as reward).

  Outro: „Die Sirene wird leiser, wird Atem, wird still. Ein ruhiges Wort nach dem anderen —
  das war die ganze Rettung. Die Seite atmet auf." Taunts (overwhelm, never menace): „Alles

  Standing law, restated because this fiction leans on it: a wrong-in-window answer only
  CLOSES the window; the pattern repeats — Die Panik never punishes, she only hurries.

  siren sweep up and back) · telegraph E950/M780/S620 (tell = the siren's PITCH names the
