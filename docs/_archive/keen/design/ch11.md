## 4 · Level design (build spec — cookbook §7 archetype "timed platforms")
- Size ~60×33 (4×3 screens), horizontal gallery with vertical tower finish.
- **NEW MECHANIC — fading platforms (W-wave engine note, small delta):** platform groups A/B
  alternate solid/faded on a **visible clock cycle** (Miragia grammar, cookbook §7): each
  phase readable **≥1.2s**, a 2-tick shimmer telegraphs every fade, the groups offset so one

  platforms, tighter phase) → boss door behind the face.

  with a job (the pendulum ferry).

  the tick, step on the tock (one parameter twist, wave-1 sprite).
- Population (E, ≤1/screen): tick-tock Tintenläufer ×2 (signature) · hopper ×1 · flyer ×1 ·
