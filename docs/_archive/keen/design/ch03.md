## 4 · Level design (build spec — cookbook §7 archetype "ship + cove vertical")
- Size ~45×40 (3×3.6 screens), tall climb: cove floor → hull → masts → cliff.
- Flow: cove beach (rolling sand, slopes) → water pools between wreck-ribs (the hazard
  teach) → hull breach door `1` → **Laderaum wing** (cargo hold, seal 1, walker guard) →
  deck via pole → the masts as shafts (poles = rigging, slide-down recovery) → crow's nest
  → door `2` → **Kartenraum wing** (the no-X map room, seal 2, hopper guard) → yardarm
  mover (a loading-crane platform ferrying the mast-to-cliff gap — its one job) → cliff
  ledge → the guardian's door in the sea-cave.
- Verbs: slopes on the dunes · poles as rigging (crow's-nest ledge ≤1 row above pole top) ·
  door pairs per interior wing · 1 mover (crane ferry) · drop-through deck planks.
- Population (E, ≤1/screen): walker (beach) ×1 · hopper (deck) ×1 · flyer (rigging) ×1 ·
  guards: walker in the cargo hold (seal 1), hopper in the map room (seal 2). M adds the
  signature sending — a Schattenwolke storm-variant that bolts in wave rhythm (doc 27
  creature table) over the pools; S adds a thief in the rigging.
- Collectibles: letters on the rigging arcs + hold shelves · 3 Glühwörter (bilge alcove,
  crow's nest, cove cave mouth) · helpers ×2 E.
- Secret: drop-through under the deck planks → bilge alcove (Glühwort) → pole back out.
